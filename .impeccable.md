## Design Context

### Users
Visitors seeking mindfulness guidance, personal transformation, and spiritual growth. They may be dealing with adversity and looking for hope, direction, and a sense of peace.

### Brand Personality
Calm, peaceful, elegant — a sanctuary of warmth and mindfulness.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Visual tone: Warm, inviting, serene
- Primary palette: Rich gold `#C79316`, light gold `#F5D78E`, pale gold `#FDE4A8`, dark brown `#3D2B0A`, black `#000000`
- Typography: Clean, readable, generous line-height
- Spacing: Generous, breathable — ample whitespace between sections
- Animations: Slow, gentle, unobtrusive (floating elements, soft fades)
- Anti-references: Harsh, corporate, overly bright or saturated designs

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth first** — every element should feel inviting, never cold or clinical
2. **Breathing room** — generous spacing and unhurried pacing
3. **Gentle motion** — animations are slow and subtle, never jarring
4. **Mindful hierarchy** — clear visual flow that guides without overwhelming
5. **Elegant simplicity** — remove anything that doesn't serve calm or clarity
